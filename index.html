improvise

docs examples guide

Improvise is a little library for creating random bodies of text from a simple JSON grammar.

You can edit any of the examples. Clicking the output label will re-evaluate the code.

You can use Improvise to do anything from adding a personality to your UI...

...to creating backstories for characters in a game.

In addition to the features shown here, Improvise also comes with filters, customisable syntax, grammar extension and runtime evaluation.

Improvise can deal with incredibly complex grammars at speed. Take a look at some of the examples for ideas of what can be achieved.
